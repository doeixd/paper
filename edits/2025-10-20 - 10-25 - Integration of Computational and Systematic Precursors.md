This edit explicitly positions Emergent Pragmatic Coherentism (EPC) as a synthesis and extension of four major computational and systematic frameworks in epistemology: Thagard's ECHO networks, Zollman's epistemic graphs, Rescher's systematicity, and Kitcher's evolutionary progress.
