## Changes Made (This Session)

### 1. Dewhurst (2021) Integration (3 surgical insertions)
- **Section 1.1 (Novelty and Positioning):** Added `dewhurst2021` to existing citation cluster with Hoel (2013).

**Rationale:** The original text could be read as making admissibility look like analyst discretion. The revision makes clear that the dynamics themselves generate the regime.

**Rationale:** Original abstract opened with Dennett, causing editors to pattern-match to "another Dennett paper" before reaching the novel contribution.
