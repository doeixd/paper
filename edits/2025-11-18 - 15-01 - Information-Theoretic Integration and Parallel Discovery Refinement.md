This integration deepens the paper's naturalistic foundations by showing how philosophical concepts (coherence, truth, objectivity) can be understood through information-theoretic mechanisms without reducing to mere physics. The Markov Blanket framework provides a bridge between philosophy of mind, epistemology, and thermodynamics, positioning EPC as genuinely interdisciplinary while maintaining philosophical rigor.

The parallel discovery refinement is crucial: it shows that objective knowledge emerges not from social agreement but from independent agents bumping against the same reality. This is a stronger realist claim than coordination-based accounts, as convergence becomes evidence of constraint structure rather than contingent social factors.
