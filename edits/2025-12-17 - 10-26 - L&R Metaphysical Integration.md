# L&R Metaphysical Integration - Edit Summary

## Changes Made

This revision integrates Ladyman & Ross's metaphysical framework (Information-Theoretic Structural Realism, Rainforest Realism, Ontic Structural Realism) while maintaining the paper's existing philosophical voice and CLAUDE.md guidelines.

### 1. Introduction (Section 1)
**Added:** Principle of Naturalistic Closure (PNC) paragraph after the existing methodological discussion.

> Added explicit adoption of PNC: rejects a priori intuition as metaphysical guide, requires unifying special sciences with fundamental physics.

**Rationale:** Strengthens methodological grounding and explicitly adopts L&R's key methodological constraint, protecting against charges of "neo-scholastic" intuition-based reasoning.

---

### 2. Section 2.1 - The Primacy of Information
**Changed:** "Existence as a persistent bounded entity" → "Existence as a persistent bounded pattern"

**Added:** *Real Patterns* language citing Dennett (1991) and Ladyman & Ross (2007), with emphasis on projectibility.

**Rationale:** Shifts terminology from "things/entities" toward "patterns," aligning with structural realism while maintaining readability.

---

### 3. Section 3.2 - Blankets Create "Things"
**Renamed:** "Blankets Create 'Things'" → "Rainforest Realism: Blankets Delineate Real Patterns"

**Added:** Explanation of Rainforest Realism—ontology as layered Real Patterns rather than sparse desert of particles.

**Rationale:** Natural fit with existing blanket-relative ontology discussion; elevates the section to engage directly with L&R's positive metaphysical position.

---

### 4. Section 4A.3 - Addressing the Hard Problem  
**Added:** OSR perspective paragraph that questions whether the Hard Problem presupposes substance metaphysics.

**Key claim:** If structure is ontologically fundamental, complete structural description may exhaust the phenomenon rather than merely correlate with it. Remains agnostic but notes explanatory gap may presuppose what structural realism rejects.

**Rationale:** Strengthens existing position without overclaiming to "dissolve" the Hard Problem. Maintains appropriate philosophical humility per CLAUDE.md.

---

### 5. Section 6.1 - The Mechanism of Emergence Revealed
**Added:** Clarification on "causation" following Russell (1913) and L&R.

**Key claim:** Fundamental physics describes functional dependencies, not causal "bangs." Macro-level causation = information-theoretic projectibility (maximizing mutual information with future states).

**Rationale:** Preempts physicist objection that framework illegitimately imports metaphysically loaded causation. Modest, defensive addition.

---

### 6. Section 7.1 - Synthesizing Information, Compression, and Truth
**Added:** PNC constraint on Apex Network.

**Key claim:** Standing Predicates achieve Apex status only by unifying special sciences without contradicting fundamental physics—not mere internal coherence or social agreement.

**Rationale:** Anchors the Apex Network in L&R's key methodological principle, protecting against charges of pragmatism or social constructivism.
