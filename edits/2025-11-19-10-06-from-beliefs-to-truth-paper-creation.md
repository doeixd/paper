Rather than treating logic as categorically different (necessary, *a priori*, foundational by nature), the paper demonstrates that logic achieved its core status through the same pragmatic filtering that creates any Standing Predicate, just operating at maximum scale across the longest timescale.

## Key Philosophical Innovations

### 1. Radical Naturalism About Logic
**Innovation**: Treating logic itself as emerged through pragmatic filtering rather than *a priori* foundation.

**Significance**: Dissolves traditional epistemological divide between logical/empirical, *a priori*/*a posteriori*, analytic/synthetic. All knowledge on same continuum, distinguished by degree not kind.

**Defense**: Logic's core status explained through maximal validation (billions of years + universal scope + perfect track record), not metaphysical privilege. Functional necessity (pragmatic lock-in) generates experienced necessity without requiring metaphysical necessity.

### 2. Functional Transformation Mechanism
**Innovation**: Explicit five-stage progression from hypothesis to infrastructure, showing status change from "being-tested" to "tool-that-tests."

**Significance**: Explains how truth is achieved rather than discovered. Predicates don't become true by corresponding to facts but by earning architectural positions through demonstrated viability.

**Defense**: Mechanism operates uniformly across all predicates (germ theory, logic, mathematics), validated through historical cases, generates testable predictions.

### 3. Architectural Coherence
**Innovation**: Redefining coherence as viability-under-pressure, resolving isolation objection without foundationalism.

**Significance**: Provides external constraint (brittleness accumulation) while maintaining holism. Coherence isn't two conditions (consistency + empirical adequacy) but unified criterion.

**Defense**: Brittleness is objective (mind-independent costs), holistic (affects entire system), and measurable (failed predictions, mounting anomalies).

### 4. Three Levels of Truth
**Innovation**: Nested hierarchy (contextual coherence → justified truth → objective truth) corresponding to validation degrees.

**Significance**: Explains how fictional systems can be coherent (Level 3) without being true (Level 2-1), how justified beliefs can be wrong (Level 2 doesn't guarantee Level 1), and what objectivity means without Platonism (convergent discovery on constraint-determined attractors).

**Defense**: Levels track substantive differences in validation degree; Level 1 convergence provides non-relativist anchor.

### 5. Differential Activation Dynamics
**Innovation**: Explicit model of which predicates activate for which experiences, with logic constantly active and peripheral predicates episodically active.

**Significance**: Explains scientific methodology (which commitments are questioned when), predicts research patterns, illuminates theory choice.

**Defense**: Matches historical evidence (quantum mechanics questioned causation but not logic), explains localization of response, predicts future patterns.

### 6. Programs as Conceptual Technology
**Innovation**: Standing Predicates as cached, reusable programs achieving computational closure.

**Significance**: Explains cumulative progress (each generation inherits infrastructure), thermodynamic optimization (caching minimizes costs), and why entrenchment serves efficiency.

**Defense**: Computer science analogy precise (logic = operating system, math = libraries, domain predicates = applications); explains revision costs through dependency analysis.
