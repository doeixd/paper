## Major Changes

### 1. Boldface Reduction (~75% reduction)

**Rationale:** The document relied heavily on boldface for emphasis, which can be visually overwhelming and reduce the impact of truly important terms. Academic philosophy papers should use boldface sparingly.

**Changes:**
- Removed boldface from nearly all section headers (e.g., "Key Insight:", "Connection to:", "Example:")
- Removed boldface from emphasis text that wasn't introducing key technical terms
- Retained boldface ONLY for: first introduction of technical terms (Markov blanket, lumpability, computational closure, ε-machine) and critical methodological warnings
- Converted many boldface headers to plain text with colons for cleaner flow

**Impact:** Document now reads more professionally, with emphasis placed strategically rather than ubiquitously. Key technical terms still stand out when first introduced.

### 2. Complex Sentence Breaking

**Rationale:** Several passages contained extremely long sentences with multiple nested clauses, making them difficult to parse on first reading. Breaking these improves flow and comprehension.

**Key Improvements:**
- Opening paragraph: Split introduction into clearer, more digestible segments
- FEP explanation (lines 34-38): Broken into shorter units explaining prediction, perception reframing, and neurobiological implementation separately
- Mangalam critique (line 120): Separated critique description from implications
- Self-as-interface passage (line 427): Divided dense paragraph into multiple shorter paragraphs
- Consciousness definition (line 350): Added qualifiers and split for precision
- Modal Determinacy explanation (line 689): Broken up to clarify the non-Platonic nature of Apex Network
- Is/ought boundary discussion (line 872): Separated descriptive vs. normative claims more clearly

**Impact:** Document now guides readers through complex ideas step-by-step rather than presenting them all at once. Maintains detail while improving accessibility.

### 3. Strategic Clarifications Added

**Rationale:** Certain philosophical claims could be misunderstood without additional context. These clarifications head off potential misreadings.

**Key Additions:**
- **Line 7:** Added note that consciousness definition is "not a circular definition but a functional characterization"
- **Line 19:** Qualified "existence requires information processing" as applying to "persistent bounded entities (rather than transient patterns)"
- **Line 163:** Reframed blanket-relativity claim to clarify: "What 'exists' as a unified entity depends on which Markov blanket configuration you employ, though not all blanket configurations succeed"
- **Line 350:** Changed from "Consciousness is..." to "Consciousness may be..." to indicate working hypothesis rather than definitive claim
- **Line 454:** Retitled section from "Engaging the Hard Problem" to "Addressing (Not Solving) the Hard Problem" to manage expectations
- **Line 687:** Added immediate clarification: "It is the pattern that necessarily crystallizes... determined by constraints rather than our beliefs about them"
- **Line 876:** Added explicit note that methodological caveat "doesn't invalidate the descriptive analysis that follows; it only limits the normative conclusions we can draw"

**Impact:** Reduces risk of reviewer confusion or misinterpretation. Shows philosophical sophistication by acknowledging limits proactively.

### 4. Prose Tightening

**Rationale:** Removed filler phrases and unnecessary wordiness while maintaining maximum detail and precision.

**Examples:**
- "here we develop the full mechanistic story" → "we develop the mechanistic story"
- "Most systems, however, are informationally transparent" → "Most systems are informationally transparent"
- "Before proceeding, we must clarify" → "We must clarify"
- "Empirical work in cognitive science" → "Cognitive science research"

**Impact:** More direct, confident prose without sacrificing nuance.

### 5. Formatting Consistency

**Changes:**
- Verified no stray line-ending characters in tables
- Ensured consistent section header formatting (sentence case with colons)
- Standardized use of parenthetical vs. colon-based clarifications
- Removed unnecessary em dashes (none found—already clean)

**Impact:** Professional, consistent presentation throughout.
