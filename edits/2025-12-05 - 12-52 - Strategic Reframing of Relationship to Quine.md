**Date:** 2025-12-05, 12:52

**Type of Edit:** Major strategic revision

## Overview

This revision fundamentally reframes the paper's relationship to Quine's epistemological framework. Based on feedback identifying a mischaracterization of Quine's "web of belief," we've repositioned EPC from "correcting" Quine to "building upon and extending" his architecture.

## Core Strategic Pivot

### Previous Framing (Problematic)
- Claimed Quine's web was "static" and needed to be made "dynamic"
- Suggested Quinean naturalism "lacked" external constraints
- Positioned "pragmatic pushback" as distinct from/superior to "recalcitrant experience"
- Framed EPC as departing from or fixing limitations in Quine's framework

### New Framing (Corrected)
- Quine provided the essential architecture: holistic webs subject to pragmatic revision in response to recalcitrant experience
- EPC describes the systems-level implications and patterns that emerge when this Quinean process operates at scale
- EPC introduces systemic brittleness as formalized diagnostics for assessing structural health
- "Pragmatic pushback" is explicitly identified as "recalcitrant experience observed at the systems level"
- Coercive overhead (C(t)) is positioned as extending beyond Quine's framework into political epistemology

## Key Philosophical Clarifications

### 1. On "Pragmatic Pushback" vs "Recalcitrant Experience"
Per user clarification, these are not distinct concepts. Pragmatic pushback is recalcitrant experience observed at the systems level. The paper now consistently reflects this.

### 2. On Isolation Objection
The isolation objection applies to internalist coherentists (BonJour, Krag, etc.), NOT to Quinean naturalism. Quine already solved this via empirical anchoring. EPC provides additional diagnostics but does not solve a problem Quine failed to address.

### 3. On Our Contribution
We are NOT:
- Making Quine's web dynamic (it already is)
- Providing external constraints Quine lacked (he already had them)
- Correcting Quine's deficiencies

We ARE:
- Describing systems-level patterns that emerge from Quinean processes
- Formalizing diagnostics (brittleness metrics) for epistemic health
- Extending into political epistemology (coercive overhead) beyond Quine's scope
- Quantifying what Quine's framework implied but did not measure

## Impact on Paper's Argument

This reframing STRENGTHENS the paper by:

1. **Positioning within established tradition:** Rather than claiming to fix a major figure, we build upon and extend his work, which is more philosophically defensible.

2. **Clarifying actual contribution:** Our real contribution is the diagnostic apparatus (brittleness metrics), not inventing external constraints or dynamism.

3. **Avoiding false objections:** Reviewers familiar with Quine would have immediately rejected claims that his web is "static" or lacks external constraints. This removes a vulnerability.

4. **Emphasizing coercive overhead:** By clarifying that Quine's framework doesn't address power and suppression, we properly highlight coercive overhead as a genuine novel contribution extending into political epistemology.

5. **Maintaining philosophical humility:** The paper now adopts appropriate epistemic humility while still making strong claims about our diagnostic contributions.
